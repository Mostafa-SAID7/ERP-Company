# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors and users of the ERP Company System. We value the participation of every individual and want to ensure that every person has a positive experience.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and inclusive of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being patient and understanding with others
- Helping others learn and grow

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Offensive comments related to gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, mental illness, neuro(a)typicality, physical appearance, body size, age, race, or religion
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following
- Harassing photography or recording
- Sustained disruption of community spaces
- Deliberate "outing" of any aspect of someone's identity without their consent
- Threats of violence or violent language directed against another person
- Advocating for, or encouraging, any of the above behavior

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces, including:

- GitHub repositories and issues
- Pull requests and code reviews
- Discussion forums and chat channels
- In-person events and conferences
- Any other spaces managed by the project

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team at [contact@we3ds.com](mailto:contact@we3ds.com).

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The project team is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.

## Consequences

Depending on the severity of the violation, consequences may include:

- A private warning from the project team
- A public warning
- Temporary or permanent ban from community spaces
- Removal of contributions
- Other actions deemed appropriate by the project team
